Guiding you from design to launch

Our team of website experts will handle the entire development lifecycle of your project, from your website idea to the web development services needed to publishing.

01

Discovery

The first phase of a good web development project is Discovery. It's the process of learning about you, your organization's goals and users, and the required features and functionality of the site.

02

Strategy

User Experience & Information Architecture. We use findings from the Discovery phase as the basis for strategy and suggestions for the site design, including site map, core features and functionality.

03

Design

In the third phase, the creative process begins with designs for a home page. We find that basic restraints give us the freedom to be very creative with background elements, imagery, spacing, and animations.

04

Programming

We mark-up the approved creatives to HTML 5, CSS, JavaScript, and suitable programming languages. All websites we create are coded as responsive websites using the latest technologies.

05

Evaluation

One of the most critical steps, where we review all facets of the site prior to pushing it live. This includes review of all functionality and content, thorough QA process, and client training.

06

Launch

With a launch plan in place, the final phase is to launch the website publicly. Once your new site is live, our team is available for ongoing website optimization and maintenance needs.
